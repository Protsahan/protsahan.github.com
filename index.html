About Protsahan

Protsahan India Foundation is a social impact organization registered under the Government of India as per The Indian Trusts Act of 1882. We have been working since early 2010 and got registered on May 12, 2010.

‘Protsahan’ is a Hindi word which means ‘Encouragement’.

Protsahan is an innovative youth based international non-profit organization that uses the creativity of Design, Art, Stories, Photography, Cinema and Theatre to bring sustainable change in lives of street and slum children facing drugs, sex abuse and disability & marginalized young people. Our strategic focus areas are that of Creative Education and Skills Development.

We use creative Game Based Modules and hold highly innovative Slum Women Panchayats to spread awareness about mother child health and nutrition in slums, Warli and Madhubani folk yore Designs to talk about sanitation and hygiene, Robotics and technology based workshops to simplify classroom education, Design and Cinema based workshops for marginalized young people and children to talk about issues of social relevance like health, sexuality and gender equality.
