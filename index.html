    • Protsahan

    (n.) Encouragement

    Project maintained by TMaYaD Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    Protsahan India is a youth led organization that has traversed a path breaking journey since 2010 as a registered non-profit organization, working under two broad heads of CREATIVE EDUCATION & SKILL DEVELOPMENT. We work at the grassroot level in urban slums and on streets in India empowering marginalized children and women from lowest income backgrounds. It all started in 2010 when a 23 year old young girl quit her corporate job and started creatively educating seven little girls from slums who were being sexually/domestically abused. Gradually youngsters across the country started supporting her and Protsahan was born. Today, we have mobilized more than 12,000 young Indians living across borders for the cause that Protsahan stands for. We started with one room creative arts school in Delhi. We have touched the lives of over a thousand children directly from streets & slums who were subjected to sexual abuse and drug abuse. We have also started programs for the marginalized slum women and artisans where we train them as design micro-entrepreneurs into making quality handicrafts, sanitary napkins etc. and are in the process of starting night schools for them. We believe in dignified earning and not blind charity.
